Your Starting Point

Most first-time voters begin here:

✅
Check Eligibility
Confirm you are 18+, Indian citizen, and resident of your constituency
⏳

Start →
⬜
Verify Electoral Roll
Confirm your name at electoralsearch.eci.gov.in before the final roll date
⬜
Receive EPIC
Download or collect your Electors Photo Identity Card
⬜
Locate Polling Booth
Find your assigned booth via the ECI Booth App or Google Maps
⬜
Cast Your Vote
Vote using the Electronic Voting Machine (EVM) at your assigned booth on election day
